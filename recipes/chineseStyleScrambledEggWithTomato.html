Chinese style scrambled egg with tomato

Description

This comforting dish of scrambled eggs features the natural sweetness of bright red tomatoes and is an easy-to-make side dish for any dinner table!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomato
  • Spring onion
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Chilli sauce
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Stir-fry spring onion and ginger
  2. Stir-fry tomatoes and egg
  3. Garnish and serve!
