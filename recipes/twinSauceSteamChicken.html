Twin sauce steam chicken

Description

My signature dish is none other than this Twin Sauce Steamed Chicken because not only is it simple and healthy, it also has an authentic taste that my family absolutely loves. A recurring favorite on our dining table, this chicken is incredibly juicy and tasty. Thanks to the prawn paste as well as the generous amount of bird’s eye chilies used, this dish is given an addictive spicy umami kick that we just can’t get enough of. Pair it with steamed rice for a yummy family feast!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bird's eye chillies
  • Fresh ginger
  • Garlic cloves
  • Sesame oil
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Steam the chicken
  4. Plate and serve
